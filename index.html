About

Abhisek Ghosh is a Pop artist whose music blends Indian classical nuances, Bengali melodies, and Western Rock & Pop energy. As a songwriter, composer, and producer, he crafts performances that leave lasting emotional impressions.

From conducting 1000 instrumentalists at the 2016 World Cultural Festival in Delhi to live radio performances with All India Radio, Abhisek’s work bridges tradition and contemporary sound.

A Berklee College of Music graduate with a Master’s in Contemporary Performance, he continues to explore music that resonates deeply, creating moments of presence and connection for every listener.
